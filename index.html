About me

Many people wonder what the difference between a masseuse and a massage therapist really is.

A massage therapy treatment differs from a "normal massage" in that I, as a therapist, examine the muscles/problem area before and after treatment. This is to try to find out where the pain/problem really comes from and if it got better after the treatment.

I have also taken some in-depth courses in deeper massage techniques such as revolving which gives a much more profound effect.

If you are just looking for a quiet and pleasant moment, I will of course arrange a relaxing treatment completely according to your needs!
